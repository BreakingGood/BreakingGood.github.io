Advanced Energy Vehicle Project
Languages

Arduino

In this project, Arduino was used to control the propellers of a custom build vehicle, including propeller spinning direction, power level, and acceleration speed.

Additional Info

Project Objective

The objective was to design a vehicle capable of travelling along a track using only propellers controlled via Arduino.

Project Goal

The goal was to produce the most energy efficient vehicle possible through both code and physical design.

Portfolio

An exhaustive portfolio of the design process can be found below, the password is CLASS33462

Project Design Philosophy

  1. Work with team to design the most power efficient vehicle out of everybody in the class (Success!)
  2. Document every single step of the process, including documentation of the entire design process and documentation of all team meeting agendas.
  3. Effectively communicate the design process to the judges at the Advanced Energy Vehicle championships.
  4. Coordinate effectively with all team members.
  5. Produce a high quality portfolio that displays the changes in the vehicle over time.
